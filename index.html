Who We Are

Since its inception, the Aitchison College Club de Français has expanded to become one of the most proactive societies in Aitchison College with hundreds of students studying the French language and working to improve the quality of French of all those they come into contact with.
Every year, the Club de Français organizes its “French Soirée” or "La Soirée Française" in recognition of the growing Global Francophone Community in Pakistan, aiming to provide an invaluable forum for young French learners. On this one night, all members of the Club de Français gather to develop their French speaking skills through a variety of theatrical and musical performances and interpersonal interactions with brilliant young minds from all around the world. In 2014, the foundation for the "Fête Internationale du Français a Aitchison College" (FIFAC), was laid as a revolutionary change to the Soirée Française, providing a platform for french speakers from all over the country and its neighbours to gather and polish their skills. This year we have taken it upon us to continue the tradition of our club and to revive FIFAC.
Overall, this will be an event that will allow students to escape the world where the only use for French is in adding another A grade to their transcripts or diversifying their Curriculum Vitae, and instead embrace the side of the French language associated with literature, drama, the performing arts and actual interaction with other French speakers. The embodiment of the French Culture, History and Nuance is the one aspect of French that is disregarded, and we aim to get in touch with the French Language holistically, being the first of our kind. This is definitely the place to be for one who appreciates the French Language in all its forms and aspects. We can't wait to see you all this fall! Au revoir and À bientôt!
